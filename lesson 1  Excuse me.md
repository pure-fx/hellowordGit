#英语笔记 #新概念英语


## Words

-  **Excuse** ***v.*** 原谅    ***n.*** 借口，理由
			*eg.* Please excuse me for being late. 请原谅我迟到了
			*eg.* Late again! What's your excuse this time? 又迟到了！你这次有什么理由/借口？
- **Excuse me** 
	1. 打扰一下；吸引别人注意
		*eg.* Excuse me , i think this pocket is yours. 
		*eg.* Excuse me, is this your car? （from. 老友记f.r.i.e.n.d.s）
	2. 借过一下
		*eg.* Excuse me, please let me through. 
	3. 用于事情发生之前，表示轻微的冒犯，也就略显失礼的时候，而非自己犯了什么错误
		*eg.* Excuse me for a moment. 失陪一下
		*eg.* Would you excuse me for a moment? 

## Grammars

- 陈述句变一般疑问句
	This is my handbag.
	-> Is this your handbag?

## Expressions

- **Pardon** 请再说一遍
	-相似的表达： come again?
				sorry?
				what?
- 表示他、她、它的代词
	- **he**
	- **she**
	- **it**
- **Is this your handbag?** 口语化表达可简化成：
	 Your handbag?
	 This is your handbag?
	 This your handbag?
- **yes?**  礼貌的应答呼唤，读的时候升调
	1. 应用场景1： 餐厅用餐
	*eg.* A: Excuse me"
	 B: Yes, sir? How can I help you? 
	 2. 应用场景2：回应呼唤，同课文中用法
	 A: Jack? 
	 B: Yes?
